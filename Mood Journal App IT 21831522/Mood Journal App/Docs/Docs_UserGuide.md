# MindFlow – AI Mood Journal – User Guide

## Getting Started

1. Open the MindFlow app.
2. The **Dashboard** shows today’s mood summary (based on your latest entry) and a quick analytics preview.

## Adding a Journal Entry

1. From the Dashboard, tap **"Add Journal Entry"**.
2. You’ll see today’s date and a text area labeled **"Write your thoughts"**.
3. Type your journal entry.
4. Tap **"Analyze My Mood"**.
   - The app runs AI-based sentiment analysis.
   - A mood result card will appear showing an emoji, mood label, and confidence bar.
5. Tap **"Save Entry"** to store it.

> The **Save Entry** button is disabled until a mood has been analyzed.

## Viewing Your Journals

1. From the Dashboard, tap **"View All Entries"**.
2. You’ll see cards for each journal entry with date, text preview, and mood emoji.
3. Tap any card to open **Entry Details**.
4. In Entry Details you can read the full text and review the mood and confidence.

### Deleting Entries

- In the **My Journals** list, swipe left on an entry to reveal **Delete**.

## Mood Analytics

1. On the Dashboard, tap the **"Last 7 days mood trend"** analytics preview card.
2. In **Mood Analytics** you’ll see:
   - A **line chart** of average mood scores for the last 7 days.
   - A **bar chart** showing the count of Positive, Neutral, and Negative days.
   - An **insights** section summarizing your dominant mood this week.

Use these insights to reflect on your emotional patterns and identify behaviors or habits that support your wellbeing.
